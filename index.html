PCHS GSA

What is GSA?

GSA, or Gender and Sexuality Alliance, is a club for LGBTQIA+ members and allies alike. We like to talk about our week, watch movies, plan activities, and just generally hang out. We're all welcoming and friendly, so don't be afraid to stop in, we will make sure you enjoy your stay. If you try it, but it's not your thing, don't worry! There's no commitment, no sign-up, no membership. You're free to drop in or skip our club freely.

Who, When, and Where?

Who - Anyone who wants to come, all are welcome and encouraged to try and attend at least one of our meetings!

When - Every other Wednesday, from 2:50pm to 3:50pm

Where - Since we are in the midst of a pandemic, in-person clubs are few and far between. So we've been meeting digitally, online. Our meet link can be found here.
